About the College

Mindanao State University – Maigo College of Education, Science and Technology (MSU-MCEST) is one of the campuses of the Mindanao State University system. It focuses on delivering high-quality instruction, research, and community extension services. The college provides programs that prepare students to become professionals in education, science, and technology, while promoting leadership, integrity, and service to society.
